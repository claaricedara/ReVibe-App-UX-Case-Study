## 🌟 The Vision
ReVibe is a community-driven platform designed to disrupt fast fashion. It combines social media elements with a circular economy, allowing users to swap, upcycle, and shop pre-loved clothing through a video-first experience.

## 🚀 Key Features
- **ReVibe Reels:** A video-first listing feed that gives a 360-degree view of garments, reducing returns and increasing trust.
- **Interactive Swapping:** A "Swipe & Save" mechanic to discover new styles and arrange trades effortlessly.
- **Live Sessions:** Real-time workshops where users can learn to upcycle and repair their clothes.

## 🎨 Design System
- **Typography:** Modern, accessible fonts paired for readability.
- **Color Palette:** Sustainable greens and vibrant accent colors to appeal to Gen Z and Millennial demographics.
- **Tools:** **Figma** for high-fidelity prototyping and **Canva** for branding and marketing assets.
